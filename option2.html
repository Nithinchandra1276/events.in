Available Loans

Home Loan

Finance your dream home with competitive interest rates and flexible repayment options. Perfect for first-time home buyers and those looking to upgrade.

Eligibility: Salaried individuals with minimum income of ₹25,000/month

Personal Loan

Quick financing for personal needs like medical emergencies, education, or debt consolidation. No collateral required with fast approval process.

Eligibility: Age 21-60 years with stable income and good credit score

Business Loan

Capital for business expansion, working capital, or starting a new venture. Flexible terms designed for entrepreneurs and business owners.

Eligibility: Business owners with minimum 2 years experience and turnover ₹5 lakhs+

Education Loan

Fund your higher education abroad or in India. Covers tuition fees, accommodation, and other educational expenses with easy repayment options.

Eligibility: Students admitted to recognized educational institutions

Car Loan

Drive home your dream car with affordable financing options. Competitive rates with flexible tenure up to 7 years.

Eligibility: Minimum age 21 years with steady income and valid driving license

Gold Loan

Instant cash against your gold jewelry. Quick approval with competitive interest rates and flexible repayment terms.

Eligibility: Gold owners aged 18+ with valid ID proof

Government Schemes & Services

PAN Card Services

Apply for new PAN card, corrections, duplicate PAN, and e-PAN. Online application with fast processing and doorstep delivery.

Eligibility: Indian citizens and entities requiring PAN for tax purposes

Aadhaar Card Services

Enroll for new Aadhaar, update demographic details, address changes, and biometric updates. Essential identity document.

Eligibility: Residents of India, including newborns and foreigners

Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana

Financial inclusion scheme providing zero-balance savings accounts, RuPay debit cards, and accident insurance coverage up to ₹1 lakh.

Eligibility: Indian citizens above 10 years of age

Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana

Loan scheme for micro and small enterprises. Provides loans up to ₹10 lakhs for business activities without collateral.

Eligibility: Small business owners and entrepreneurs

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana

Affordable housing scheme providing financial assistance for construction/ purchase of houses for economically weaker sections.

Eligibility: Annual income below ₹6 lakhs for urban areas

Atal Pension Yojana

Guaranteed pension scheme for unorganized sector workers. Provides monthly pension between ₹1,000 to ₹5,000 after retirement.

Eligibility: Age 18-40 years with bank account

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana

Crop insurance scheme protecting farmers against crop loss due to natural calamities, pests, and diseases.

Eligibility: All farmers growing notified crops in notified areas

Stand Up India Scheme

Loan scheme for SC/ST and women entrepreneurs. Provides loans between ₹10 lakhs to ₹1 crore for setting up greenfield enterprises.

Eligibility: SC/ST or women entrepreneurs above 18 years

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi

Direct income support scheme for farmers providing ₹6,000 per year in three equal installments to farmer families.

Eligibility: Small and marginal farmers with landholding up to 2 hectares

Ayushman Bharat Yojana

Health insurance scheme providing coverage up to ₹5 lakhs per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization.

Eligibility: Families identified in SECC database and other identified families

Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana

LPG gas connection scheme providing free LPG connections to women from BPL households to reduce health hazards from cooking fuels.

Eligibility: Women above 18 years from BPL households

Swachh Bharat Mission

Clean India initiative focusing on sanitation, waste management, and behavioral change regarding healthy sanitation practices.

Eligibility: All Indian citizens, with incentives for rural households

Digital India Programme

Programme to transform India into a digitally empowered society and knowledge economy through technology initiatives.

Eligibility: All Indian citizens and businesses

Make in India Initiative

Flagship initiative to transform India into a global manufacturing hub by encouraging companies to manufacture in India.

Eligibility: Domestic and foreign companies interested in manufacturing

GST Registration & Services

Goods and Services Tax registration, amendments, returns filing, and compliance services for businesses.

Eligibility: Businesses with turnover above threshold limits

Startup India Initiative

Programme to promote entrepreneurship by creating a conducive ecosystem for startups to grow and generate employment.

Eligibility: Innovative startups with scalable business models

Skill India Mission

National skill development mission to train youth in industry-relevant skills and provide employment opportunities.

Eligibility: Youth aged 15-35 years across India

Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana

Skill development scheme providing short-term training programs to youth for employment in various sectors.

Eligibility: Unemployed youth aged 18-35 years

MGNREGA (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA)

Employment guarantee scheme providing 100 days of guaranteed wage employment to rural households.

Eligibility: Rural households willing to do unskilled manual work

Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana

Maternity benefit scheme providing ₹5,000 to pregnant women and lactating mothers for better nutrition.

Eligibility: Pregnant women above 19 years, first living child

National Pension Scheme (NPS)

Voluntary retirement savings scheme regulated by PFRDA for long-term savings and retirement income.

Eligibility: Indian citizens aged 18-65 years

Public Provident Fund (PPF)

Long-term savings scheme providing tax benefits and guaranteed returns for retirement planning.

Eligibility: Indian residents, HUFs, and associations
